Steps How to make pancakes according to the classic recipe? Eggs should be broken into a cup separately to check if they are fresh. Then mix the eggs in a deep bowl. Add granulated sugar and salt to the eggs. Beat the mixture with a mixer, whisk or fork until it is completely mixed. Pour the milk into the egg mixture bowl and whisk the contents of the bowl again until smooth. Quench the soda with vinegar and pour into the mixture. Sift the flour into a separate bowl, then gradually add it to the dough. Whisk after each addition so that no lumps form. Pour vegetable oil into the dough and mix. The dough is ready. Place the frying pan on high heat, prepare a silicone brush for greasing the baking pan with vegetable oil or a piece of bacon (for the same purposes). Take a ladle or large spoon, with which to pour the dough into the pan. The dough will turn out very liquid. Reduce the heat under the pan slightly. Grease a hot baking sheet with a minimum amount of vegetable oil, pour a ladle of dough and rotate the pan in a circle, evenly distributing the dough over its surface. Place the pan on the fire. When holes appear on the top of the pancake and the batter becomes matte and solid, carefully lift the pancake with a silicone spatula and quickly flip it over. This must be done carefully, as the pancakes are very thin and break easily. Bake all the pancakes in this way. Serve with cream, butter and sugar or jam.
